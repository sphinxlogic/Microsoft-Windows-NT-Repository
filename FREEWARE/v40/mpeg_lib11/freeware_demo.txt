Sources and object libraries are located under [.SRC] directory in
MPEGLIB-11-VMS.ZIP file. You can extract them with UNZIP utility.

Files needed for compiling and linking:

MPEG.H
LIBMPEG.OLB            (built with VAXC 3.2 on VAX/VMS)
LIBMPEG.OLB_DECC       (built with DEC C 4.0 on VAX/VMS 6.0)
LIBMPEG.OLB_AXP        (built with DEC C 4.0 on Alpha/VMS 6.1)
