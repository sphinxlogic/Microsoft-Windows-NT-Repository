VGP 1.1 under OpenVMS 
=====================

You can run a demo (VGP drawing an Earth map) via FREEWARE_DEMO.COM  procedure.

Sources are into [.SRC] directory. AXPOBJS.ZIP and VAXOBJS.ZIP archives contain
object files for relinking if you don't have a C compiler.
