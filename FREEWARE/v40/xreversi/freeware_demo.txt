XREVERSI port under OpenVMS:
===========================

Ressource file REVERSI.DAT must be either under SYS$LOGIN: or under
DECW$SYSTEM_DEFAULTS:


You can run XREVERSI via FREEWARE_DEMO.COM procedure

Sources are available under [.SRC] directory in XREVERSI.ZIP file.
