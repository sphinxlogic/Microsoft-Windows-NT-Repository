You can run XFORTRESS via FREEWARE_DEMO.COM procedure

Sources are available under [.SRC] directory in XFORTRESS.ZIP file.

To rebuild:

You need DECUS X11R4 (by Patrick L. Mahan, TGV) includes to compile XFORTRESS
and DECUS X11R4 libraries do relink. You can get them at your local DECUS 
office.

You can get libraries (XAW, XMU and XVMSUTILS) at ftp servers 
ada.cenaath.cena.dgac.fr and ftp2.cnam.fr in [.DECWINDOWS]
directory. Look at BRIEF.DESCRIPTION file in the [ANONYMOUS.DECWINDOWS] 
directory.
