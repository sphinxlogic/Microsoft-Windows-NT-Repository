XISOLA game for OpenVMS VAX & AXP   - Feb 1995
=================================

You can run XISOLA via FREEWARE_DEMO.COM procedure

Sources are available under [.SRC] directory in XISOLA.ZIP file.

Rebuild:

Xisola needs XAW, XMU and XVMSUTILS libraries from X11R4 DECUS distribution.
You can get them from ftp serveurs ftp2.cnam.fr and ada.cenaath.cena.dgac.fr
in [.DECWINDOWS] directory.

OBJs and EXEs are provided for VAX & AXP (VAX VMS 5.5-2 with MOTIF 1.1 and VAX
C 3.2, AXP VMS 6.1 with MOTIF 1.2 and DEC C 4.0).

Enjoy !!
