XSPRINGIES port under OpenVMS  VAX & AXP    -   March 1995
========================================

You can run XSPRINGIES via FREEWARE_DEMO.COM procedure. You must define your
own masses and springies, select a force field and GO !! 

Sources are available under [.SRC] directory in ---.ZIP fil

A lot of saved systems are available into [.LIB] directory of the ZIP file.
They may be pointed by XSP_DIR logical name to be loaded interactively when
running Xspringies.
