VMS port of SPATIAL and SPHERAL.        March 1994
================================

You can run SPATIAL via FREEWARE_DEMO.COM procedure

By default, score files are under SYS$LOGIN and shape files under CENA_X
(this is the localization of X stuff at CENA). CENA_X is defined by
FREEWARE_DEMO.COM procedure. 

Sources are available under [.SRC] directory in THREDEE.ZIP file
