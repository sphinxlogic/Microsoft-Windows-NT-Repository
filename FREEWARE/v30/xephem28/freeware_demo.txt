XEPHEM 2.8 under OpenVMS
========================

You can run Xephem via FREEWARE_DEMO.COM procedure.

Sources (and stars databases) are located into [.SRC] directory. AXPOBJS.ZIP
and VAXOBJS.ZIP archives contain object files for relink if you don't have a C
compiler.
