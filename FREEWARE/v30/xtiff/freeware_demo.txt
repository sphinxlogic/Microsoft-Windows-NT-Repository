XTIFF 2.0 for OpenVMS VAX & AXP - Feb 1995
===============================

OBJS and EXES are provided (VAX/VMS 5.5-2 with Motif 1.1 and VAX C 3.2,
AXP/VMS 6.1 with Motif 1.2 and DEC C 4.0)

You can run XTIFF via FREEWARE_DEMO.COM procedure, and you can display a slide
of Valloire (a village in the French Alpes).

Sources are available under [.SRC] directory in XTIFF.ZIP file
