XGAS under OpenVMS
==================

You can run Xgas via FREEWARE_DEMO.COM procedure.

Sources are located into [.SRC] directory. AXPOBJS.ZIP and VAXOBJS.ZIP archives
contain object files for relink if you don't have a C compiler.
