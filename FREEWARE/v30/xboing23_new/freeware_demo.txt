XBOING V 2.3 (New) port under OpenVMS VAX & AXP   March 96
===============================================

You can run XBOING23 via FREEWARE_DEMO.COM procedure


Sources are available under [.SRC] directory. AXPOBJS.ZIP and VAX_GCC_OBJS.ZIP
contain object files for relinking. You can also rebuild with DEC C (from
versions 4.0 to 5.2)  on VAX and ALpha, or GCC on VAX (tested with GCC 2.5.8)

XPM object libraries are provided for the 2 compilers. VAX (GCC 2.5.8 under VMS
5.5-2) and AXP (DEC C 4.0 under VMS 6.1) are provided.

Define XBOING23_DIR somewhere and put level files here (all the content of
       ^^^^^^^^^^^^
[.LEVELS] directory

On alphas, it may be worth to invoke Xboing with -sync and -speed 1 
to slow dow the game ...
