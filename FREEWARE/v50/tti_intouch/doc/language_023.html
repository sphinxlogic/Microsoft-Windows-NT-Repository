Appendix B
Reserved Words

Reserved words are words that cannot be used as identifiers. INTOUCH's reserved words are:

  • DATE
  • DATE$
  • ELSE
  • EXLINE
  • EXTYPE
  • MAXNUM
  • NOT
  • PI
  • PRINT
  • REM
  • RND
  • TIME
  • TIME$

The following words will be reserved in future versions:

  • CON
  • IDN
  • NUL$
  • TRANSFORM
  • ZER
